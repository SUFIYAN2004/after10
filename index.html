Welcome to the Path Selector

This simple tool helps you determine the educational path based on your subject marks. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter your marks for each subject (Language, English, Science, Social, Maths) in the provided fields.
  2. Click on the "Calculate Path" button.
  3. The tool will calculate the average percentage of your marks.
  4. Based on the percentage, it will recommend one of the following paths: Computer Science, Biology, or Commerce.
